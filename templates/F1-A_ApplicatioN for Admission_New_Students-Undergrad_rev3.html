DATA PRIVACY STATEMENT AND AGREEMENT (DATA PRIVACY ACT OF 2012)
The Office of the Guidance, Counseling, and Admission (OGCA) is committed to protecting the privacy of its data
subjects, and ensuring the safety and securing of personal data under its control and custody. The OGCA collects, stores and
processes personal data from its current, past and prospective students starting with the information provided during
application for admission.
Furthermore, the information collected and stored by the Unit shall only be used for the following purposes: Processing
of admission application and student selection, Verifying authenticity of student records and documents, Supporting the students
well-being and providing guidance counselling, Documentation of students data, Management of data records for active
population, and as Basis for further development of service protocols and guidelines.
